Elena G Lara



Elena is a PhD student in Biostatistics at Radboud UMC, with a background in Bioinformatics and Statistics. Funded by the SIMPATHIC Horizon Europe project, she works on developing advanced clinical trials for drug repurposing in rare diseases. In particular, her research focuses on basket trials with a crossover design and individualized outcome measures. The work is carried out under the supervision of Kit Roes, Joanna in 't Hout and Steven Teerenstra.
