Laurens Sluijterman

Laurens is a researcher in the Biostatistics unit at Radboud UMC. His research focuses on developing a regulatory evaluation framework for the INVENTS project. This European project leverages computer models and innovative trial designs to tackle the challenges of pharmaceutical research for rare diseases. His other interests include machine learning, in particular uncertainty quantification for neural networks.
